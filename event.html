Date: 28th March 2016-31st Dec 2017
Time: 9AM-12AM
Location: Al Wakrah,Qatar
Entrance: Children of age below 3 are free
General

SHARE

ABOUT THE EVENT

80 to 100 kms away from Doha Arabian Adventures Qatar Camp is located in the middle of the desert and directly by the beach. The journey starts from Doha heading south towards Sea Line Beach Resort. Our drivers will stop at the Sealine, the entrance to the golden dunes to deflate the tyers and the there onward our professional desert drivers will drive you into the deserts and over the Golden Dunes till you reach Arabian Adventures Qatar. Swimming, Beach Volleyball, Football and Sand Boarding are part of our activities. BBQ or traditional Arabic food, accompanied by your choice of soft drinks, is prepared on site and will be served while you are enjoying your time at the camp.
